Aussie BioDiesel

Turning waste into fuel.

We start by collecting used cooking oil from homes and businesses, with a long-term goal of expanding to suitable organic waste from farms and markets to support biodiesel production for transport, generators and energy use.

How it works Work with us

Collect

Scheduled pickup of waste cooking oil and foods.

Process

Filtering, sorting, dewatering, chemical processing and quality checks.

Supply

Distribution to customers to use in tranport and energy production.

Why biodiesel

  • Turns waste into value: As of a 2025 finding 86,000 tonns of grease trap waste was recovered from landfil and 256,000 tonnes of farm waste went to landfil.
  • Practical sustainability: Waste such as cooking oils and biomass can be reprocessed into usable biofuels.

Who we work with

Restaurants, commercial kitchens, farms and markets

We provide bins and a pickup schedule for waste cooking oil collection.

domestic customers

A physical location for domestic customers to drop off household waste and purchase fuel.
