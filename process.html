How it works

Our model is simple: collect waste cooking oils and select produce, sort and refine it into biodiesel using traditional chemical processes and then supply it to customers locally.

Step-by-step

  1. Collection: we agree on a pickup schedule and container setup for your site.
  2. Pre-treatment and sort: we sort and remove food particles and water to improve consistency.
  3. Production: oil is processed into biodiesel using a chemical process with methanol and sodium hydroxide. While farm and food waste is processed and turned into a slurry before being separted into oil
  4. Testing, refinement & storage: testing is done and further refinement if necessary before the fuel is stored and distributed.

Quality & safety

  • We aim to follow relevant Australian fuel-quality expectations for biodiesel and safe chemical handling.
  • Batch records and basic testing help ensure consistent output and quality.
  • Storage and transport follow safe handling practices and appropriate labelling.
