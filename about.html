About the ABD

Aussie BioDiesel is focused on turning cooking oil, farm and produce waste into biodiesel to support cleaner, more local energy options in Australia.

Our mission

Make biodiesel more accessible by building a simple supply chain: partner with businesses for feedstock, run batch processing and distribute locally to reduce transport and waste.
